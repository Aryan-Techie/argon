ARGON Privacy Policy

At ARGON, we take your privacy seriously.
This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled when you use our browser extension.

Your Data Stays Local

ARGON is designed with a strong commitment to privacy:

  • All data is stored 100% locally on your device
  • No data is ever sent to our servers
  • No analytics or telemetry is collected
  • No accounts or logins required

Information Storage

ARGON stores the following information locally:

  • Your personal settings and preferences
  • Browsing data related only to sites you've chosen to manage
  • Usage statistics (solely for your own reference)

How Your Information is Used

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is used solely to provide the extension's functionality
  • No one but you has access to your data
  • Your data is automatically erased if you uninstall the extension

No Data Sharing

We cannot and do not share your data with third parties because:

  • We never receive your data in the first place
  • All operations occur entirely within your browser
  • No server communication occurs during normal operation

Browser Permissions

ARGON requires certain browser permissions to function:

  • Access to tab information (to identify distracting sites)
  • Storage permission (to save your preferences locally)
  • These permissions are used only to provide core functionality

Your Rights

Since all data is local, you maintain complete control:

  • View your data anytime through the extension's interface
  • Delete your data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling
  • Export any stored data using the extension's built-in tools

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@aroice.in.
