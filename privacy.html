ARGON Privacy Policy

At ARGON, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

ARGON collects minimal data to provide our productivity and focus tools:

  • Browsing activity only on sites you've chosen to track or block
  • Usage statistics of the extension's features
  • Settings and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide core functionality (site blocking, focus tracking)
  • Generate personalized productivity insights
  • Improve the extension based on usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored:

  • Primarily locally on your device
  • Optionally in your browser's sync storage (if enabled)
  • We employ industry-standard security measures to protect any data stored remotely

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • When required by law
  • If you explicitly opt into sharing specific information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of any optional data collection

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes via the extension or our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@aroice.in.
