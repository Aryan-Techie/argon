ARGON Privacy Policy

At ARGON, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled when you use our Chrome extension for productivity and digital wellbeing.

Privacy-First Design

ARGON is designed with a strong commitment to privacy:

  • 100% Local Storage: All data is stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's local storage API
  • No External Servers: No data is ever transmitted to external servers or cloud services
  • No User Tracking: No analytics, telemetry, or user behavior tracking of any kind
  • No Accounts Required: No registration, login, or account creation needed
  • No Ads: Completely ad-free experience with no advertising networks

Data We Store Locally

ARGON stores the following information locally on your device:

Website Blocking Data

  • Your list of blocked websites and domains
  • Website blocking preferences and settings
  • Temporary access permissions and timers

Screen Time Analytics

  • Daily and weekly browsing time statistics
  • Time spent on individual websites
  • Hourly usage patterns and trends
  • Top visited websites data

Productivity Features

  • Pomodoro timer sessions and history
  • Focus session statistics and preferences
  • Doom scroll intervention records
  • Customizable timer durations and settings

Digital Wellbeing

  • Health reminder preferences and schedules
  • Posture, hydration, and eye care reminder settings
  • Movement and breathing exercise preferences
  • Custom reminder intervals and notifications

User Interface

  • Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Dashboard layout and customization settings
  • Extension popup configurations

Browser Permissions Required

ARGON requires specific browser permissions to function effectively:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and statistics locally
  • WebNavigation: To monitor and block distracting websites
  • Tabs: To access current tab information for blocking functionality
  • Alarms: To schedule health reminders and productivity timers
  • Notifications: To display wellbeing reminders and productivity alerts
  • Scripting: To inject doom scroll detection and intervention scripts

These permissions are used exclusively to provide the extension's core functionality and are never used to collect or transmit personal data.

How Your Information is Used

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is used solely to provide ARGON's productivity and wellbeing features
  • No one but you has access to your data
  • Data is processed locally within your browser environment
  • Your data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the extension
  • No data profiling or behavioral analysis occurs on external servers

No Data Sharing

We cannot and do not share your data with third parties because:

  • We never receive your data in the first place
  • All operations occur entirely within your browser
  • No server communication occurs during normal operation
  • No third-party analytics services are integrated
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with advertising networks

Data Security

Your data security is ensured through:

  • Local storage using Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • No transmission of sensitive data over networks
  • Browser-level encryption for stored data
  • No external attack vectors through server connections

Your Rights and Control

Since all data is local, you maintain complete control:

  • View Your Data: Access all stored data through the extension's dashboard
  • Delete Your Data: Clear data by resetting extension settings or uninstalling
  • Export Data: Use built-in tools to export your productivity statistics
  • Modify Settings: Change or update any stored preferences at any time
  • Granular Control: Enable or disable specific features independently

Open Source Transparency

ARGON is an open-source project:

  • Source code is publicly available on GitHub
  • You can review exactly how your data is handled
  • Community auditing ensures privacy practices are maintained
  • Licensed under the MIT License for transparency

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any material changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Communicated through the extension's update mechanism
  • Highlighted in our project documentation
