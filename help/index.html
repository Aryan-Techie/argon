Installation Guide

Installing ARGON in your Chrome browser is simple and takes less than a minute.

1

Visit the Chrome Web Store

Open the Chrome Web Store in your browser.

The Chrome Web Store home page

2

Search for ARGON

Type "ARGON Focus" in the search bar and press Enter.

Note: ARGON is currently in development and will be available in the Chrome Web Store soon. You can join our waitlist to be notified when it launches.

3

Add to Chrome

Click the "Add to Chrome" button and confirm the installation when prompted.

4

Pin ARGON to Your Toolbar

For easy access, pin ARGON to your Chrome toolbar:

  1. Click the puzzle icon (extensions) in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Find ARGON in the dropdown list
  3. Click the pin icon to pin it to your toolbar

Pro Tip: Pinning ARGON to your toolbar makes it easier to start focus sessions with just one click.

Initial Setup & Configuration

After installation, ARGON will guide you through a quick setup process to customize your experience. Here's what to expect:

1

Welcome Screen

Click the ARGON icon in your toolbar to launch the setup wizard.

2

Set Your Focus Goals

Tell ARGON what you're trying to accomplish with your focus time. This helps the AI better understand your needs.

3

Identify Distractions

Select websites that typically distract you or add your own custom sites to the blocking list.

4

Set Your Schedule

Configure your typical work hours and when you'd like ARGON to be most active.

5

Permissions Setup

ARGON will request the permissions it needs to function properly. Review and accept these to complete setup.

Important: ARGON needs website access permissions to function correctly. These permissions are used solely for the purpose of blocking distractions and never to collect your personal data.

Yes! If you create an ARGON account, your settings and statistics will sync across all your devices where ARGON is installed. This allows you to maintain a consistent productivity environment whether you're working on your personal laptop, work computer, or any other device.
