Description of Service

ARGON is a productivity-focused Chrome extension designed to help users:

  • Block Distracting Websites: Smart site blocking with custom domain filtering and temporary access options
  • Track Screen Time: Monitor daily and weekly browsing patterns with detailed analytics
  • Prevent Doom Scrolling: Intelligent detection and intervention for excessive scrolling behavior
  • Enhance Focus: Built-in Pomodoro timer with customizable work and break sessions
  • Promote Digital Wellbeing: Health reminders for posture, hydration, eye care, and movement
  • Provide Modern Interface: Clean, intuitive dashboard with dark theme and smooth animations

The extension operates completely locally on your device without requiring internet connectivity for its core functionality.
